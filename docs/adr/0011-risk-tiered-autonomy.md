# Risk Tiered Autonomy Is the Default Execution Policy

Forgelet will not be fully autonomous and will not ask for approval before every step. It will use Risk Tiered Autonomy: low-risk reads, analysis, planning, and reversible model output can proceed automatically; durable writes, model escalation, local commands, knowledge persistence, and external effects require confirmation according to policy; destructive or secret-touching actions are denied or strongly confirmed. This preserves flow for everyday work while keeping durable and risky effects visible.
